What We Do?

Premium Washing Services

Exterior Washing

Soft washing is a special exterior cleaning method that uses low-pressure water and a mix of biodegradable cleaners to safely remove dirt, mold, algae, and mildew from a building's surfaces.

Interior Washing

Special interior washing refers to a deep, often specialized cleaning service that goes beyond regular housekeeping to address unique challenges like post-construction cleanup, high-level dusting, and sanitation of specific areas with specialized equipment and techniques.

Vacuum Cleaning

Special vacuum cleaning refers to the use of specialized vacuums and techniques to clean specific environments or materials, such as HEPA vacuums for fine dust and allergens, industrial vacuums for heavy debris, or wet/dry vacuums for liquid spills.

Seats Washing

Special seat washing is a deep cleaning process for upholstery that uses specialized equipment like spray-extraction cleaners, steam cleaners, or professional-grade cleaning solutions to remove stubborn stains, dirt, and odors from car seats, sofas, and other furniture.

Window Wiping

Special window wiping involves using professional-grade tools and techniques to thoroughly clean windows, removing stubborn grime, hard water spots, and streaks for a crystal-clear finish.

Wet Cleaning

Special wet cleaning is an environmentally friendly alternative to dry cleaning that uses water and biodegradable detergents in a gentle, computer-controlled process to clean delicate and "dry clean only" garments without harsh chemical solvents.

Oil Changing

Special oil changing involves using high-quality, manufacturer-recommended oils and filters, often for high-performance or specific vehicle types, and may include a more thorough inspection of the vehicle's engine and fluid systems.

Brake Reparing

Special brake repairing is a comprehensive service that goes beyond simple pad replacement to diagnose and fix complex issues within the entire brake system, including the calipers, rotors, master cylinder, and ABS system, often using specialized tools and techniques for optimal safety and performance.
